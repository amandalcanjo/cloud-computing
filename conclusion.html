Conclusion

Conclusion

The Covid-19 pandemic accelerated this task virtualization process, modifying the new normal and tasks that were previously performed in person can now be performed from anywhere in the world without any loss of quality.

Despite becoming popular in the 2000s, cloud computing has been gaining more space in the market and has become a trend affecting all sectors. Datacenters are becoming not just IT companies but partners with large, medium and small businesses.

Every year, more companies adopt cloud computing to stay competitive in the globalized market. Despite bringing several benefits to professionals, such as outsourcing the IT sector and reducing costs while keeping the total focus on the enterprise, cloud computing also brings us a concern regarding the confidentiality of data stored in the cloud. Technology companies suffer more and more cyber attacks with each passing day, and information leaks cause great concern to people.

This is a problem faced by data centers that every day implement high security standards to ensure that these inconveniences do not occur. As we can see in this article, cloud computing is here to stay, pushing the technology sector to other levels and leveraging other sectors of the economy.
